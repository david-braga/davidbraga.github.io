EMOTIONAL INTELLIGENCE

* What's Emotional Intelligence?

Is the ability to recognize, understand, manage and deal with your own and other's emotions.

*How is it different to IQ?

While IQ can be measured by a score comparison with other individuals that have taken the IQ test and are in the same age group's result, EQ is more related to emotion-based problem solving.

* Why is it important?

Measuring the IQ is not the only way to find out if someone will succeed in life as it was believed in the past. Nowadays more companies use EQ tests as part of their hiring process, since researches found that good leaders had higher emotional intelligence.
